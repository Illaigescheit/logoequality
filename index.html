What is the Logo Equality Project?

The Logo Equality project was born after the decision of The United States Supreme Court's landmark ruling
to allow same-sex marriage in all 50 states.

The celebration of Progress and Equality and it also brought up the spirit of Design and Creativity from startups, enterprises and other institution that redesigned their own rainbow colour brand logo to support equality
in the world. This list comes to show to the world the amount of support and creativity that this brave
and wise decision led to.
